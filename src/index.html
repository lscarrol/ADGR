ABOUT US

Armienti, DeBellis, Guglielmo & Rhoden, LLP has over twenty (20) years of experience as high quality litigators, trial attorneys and counselors at law for diverse and demanding clients. Our practice has grown on the principles of calculated aggressiveness, attention to detail, experience and cost efficiency. Our attorneys have substantial background, built upon extensive experience, in a wide variety of tort defense matters.

PARTNERS

Mike Armienti

Born New York, New York; admitted to bar, New York (1980), Florida (1982) and U.S. District Court, Southern and Eastern Districts of New York (1980). Education: Hofstra University (B.B.A., summa cum laude); Pace University (J.D.); Assistant District Attorney, New York County and Office of Special Narcotics Prosecutor. Member of the Board of Directors of Executive Insurance Company. Lecturer: Cross examination techniques of medical experts. Honors/Awards: The American Society of Legal Advocates’ 2015 Top 100 Insurance Lawyer in the State of New York. Member: New York State Insurance Association (Education & Conference Planning Committee); Risk and Insurance Management Society, Inc.; New York Claims Association; New York State Trial Lawyers Association; New York State Bar Association; Bronx County Bar Association.
